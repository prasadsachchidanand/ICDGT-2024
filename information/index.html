Important Information

Weather:

The current temperature in Changchun is between -7°C and -17°C. Please ensure to bring warm clothing and prepare for the cold winter weather.

Restricted Services in China:

Services like Google, WhatsApp, YouTube, Gmail, and Telegram may not work in China. If you have an international roaming plan, these services should be accessible. We recommend setting up alternatives beforehand.

Install Alipay:

Alipay is essential for making payments in China. You can link your Visa or MasterCard to Alipay for easy transactions at shops, restaurants, and other services. It’s widely accepted across China.
