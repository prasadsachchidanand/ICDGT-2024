International Conference on Differential Geometry and Topology

12-15 December, 2024

Jilin University, Changchun, China

The Conference on Differential Geometry and Topology aims to convene leading researchers and academics to explore the latest advancements in these pivotal fields. Featuring a prestigious selection of keynote speakers and specialized workshops, the event is designed to encourage intellectual exchange and foster collaborative research within the mathematical community.

Highlight: Mini-Course on $K$-Theory

A special feature of the conference is a mini-course on $K$-theory by Prof. Thomas Schick from Universität Göttingen. This mini-course will provide an in-depth introduction to K-theory, a cornerstone of modern mathematics with profound applications in geometry and topology. It's an unmissable opportunity for researchers and students to learn from a renowned expert in the field.
